About Me!

I'm Reham, a stay-at-home mom who is passionate about coding.
My journey started 7 months ago when I decided to start learning something I love while managing my house.
I dedicated a full time self-learning schedule to start learning the basics.
In those 7 months, I've been able to learn SQL, R, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and Git along with other non-coding technical skills like Advanced Excel and Google Sheets.
Although I used to love my previous jobs in the banking sector, I've always felt that this was not where I belong.
My passion for computer and technology drove me all the way to this path and I'm really excited for the next step of my journey!
